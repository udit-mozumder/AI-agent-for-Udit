Overview

Sonapur is a semi-urban locality located on the outskirts of Guwahati in the northeastern state of Assam. It lies along the important NH27 highway, acting as a gateway between Guwahati and upper Assam regions. Over time, Sonapur has grown from a quiet rural belt into a developing suburban hub.

🗺️ Location & Connectivity
Situated ~20–25 km from Guwahati city center
Located on National Highway 27 (NH27)
Well connected via:
Road transport (buses, shared cabs, private vehicles)
Nearby railway access through Guwahati
Close to key transit routes connecting to Meghalaya and upper Assam
🌄 Geography & Environment
Surrounded by lush green hills, forests, and small rivers
Part of the Brahmaputra valley ecosystem
Pleasant climate with:
Heavy rainfall during monsoon
Mild winters
Rich biodiversity typical of Northeast India
